Welcome to Pro Recorder

If your screens are not showing up or audio is failing, please grant OS permissions.

🍎 macOS Users

  • Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security.
  • In Screen Recording, enable the toggle for this app.
  • Do the same for Microphone.
  • Restart the app for changes to take effect.

🪟 Windows Users

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & security.
  • In App permissions, turn ON Camera and Microphone access for desktop apps.

🎧 System Audio (Mac)

To record meeting participants on Mac, use a virtual audio cable like BlackHole. Create a Multi-Output Device and select BlackHole in this app.
